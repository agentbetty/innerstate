Client

GHeL provides online courses and certificate programs for global health professionals.

I expanded their underutilized brand with a fresh color palette and strategy alongside a new, vibrant visual refresh. We also implemented course and certificate recommendations, faceted searches, card filtering, and an upgraded dashboard to support these health professionals in their learning.

Alongside extending the brand and creating a refresh, I worked with a team to flesh out wire frames for a holistic and intuitive user experience.
