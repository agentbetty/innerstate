About Me

I maintain a strong, formal design sensibility alongside a love for how things are built, how we manage that building process, and how we keep it all centered on the user.

As user experience matures, users are less enticed by feature-centric products: they seek value and a higher quality experiences. We need to consider outcome (worth) at the same time as output (performance).

That requires a rethinking and retooling of the development and design philosophies. That requires the features we provide to not only be vetted, stable, and quickly available, but also to be informative, entertaining, and meaningful.
